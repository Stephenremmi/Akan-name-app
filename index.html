Finding out your exact day of birth can be a bit hectic especially with the day checker formula??With the invention of this web app, count yourself lucky!!.
This web application provides a simple user interface easy to use.This web app outputs the day of the week you were born in Akan name depending on your gender but likewise you can refer to exact day from the list below.
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
